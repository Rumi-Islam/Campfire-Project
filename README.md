# Sewer Escape 3d
You fall into the sewers, and are taken deep within. Escape the sewers to win.
In this game, you move around through a mazelike environment that uses a flashlight.
Don't worry though: you are great at cartography, and are able to make a map to figure out where to go!
Move throughout the maze and do tasks to help you escape, such as fixing wires, pipes, and codes.

A/D/Left arrow/Right arrow - turn left or right.
W/Up arrow - Move forward.
S/Down arrow - Look at map.
Click and hold to wires to fix them.
Click and turn wheels to fix pipes.
Click the numbers and paper to solve code.
